## Evaluation Framework

### Criteria for Source Assessment

**Academic Credibility (Weight: 30%)**
- Peer-reviewed status
- Journal/conference reputation
- Author institutional affiliation
- Citation count and impact

**Relevance to Topic (Weight: 25%)**
- Direct applicability to Physical AI
- Alignment with humanoid robotics concepts
- Coverage of mechanical, electrical, control, or AI layers
- Suitability for graduate-level audience

**Recency (Weight: 20%)**
- Publication date relative to rapid field advancement
- Balance between foundational and recent work
- Availability of updated versions or follow-up studies

**Methodology Quality (Weight: 15%)**
- Reproducibility of results
- Rigorous experimental design
- Validated evaluation metrics
- Clear limitations acknowledged

**Accessibility (Weight: 10%)**
- Availability to target audience
- Clarity of presentation
- Adequate technical depth without being overly complex
